What This Section Covers

In the current prototype, the patient module focuses on a simple registration flow, basic personal information, and a table for reviewing saved records.

  • Patient registration starts with DNI, full name, and email.
  • The information is intended to be linked to the patient's identity.
  • The records page helps present the information in an organized table.
